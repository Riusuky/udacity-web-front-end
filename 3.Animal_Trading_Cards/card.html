Rhinoceros beetles

Some species have been anecdotally claimed to lift up to 850 times their own weight.

  • Family: Scarabaeidae
  • Life stages: Larval, pupae and adult
  • Adult Lifespan: 2-3 years
  • Habitat: Rain forests

The Dynastinae or rhinoceros beetles are among the largest of beetles, reaching more than 150 mm (6 in) in length, but are completely harmless to humans because they cannot bite or sting.
